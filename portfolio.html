DC Bucket List

An interactive app based on Washingtonian's Great Washington Bucket List that allows users to apply filters to the bucket list items by attributes such as price and Metrorail accessibility, making it easier to navigate and plan adventures in Washington, D.C.

Stack: (DC Bucket List is a MEAN app.)

  • AngularJS
  • Node.js
  • MongoDB
  • Mongoose
  • Express.js

Gobblr

A Thanksgiving-themed recipe manager built with Ruby on Rails. Users have the ability to add and edit recipes, to plan meals around ingredients they already have, and to plan grocery shopping around meals consisting of starred recipes.

Stack:

  • Rails
  • Devise
  • CanCanCan

GA Hacker Helper

(Group project)

GA Hacker Helper is a collaborative effort built after learning Node.js and Express as a place where General Assembly students could share resources and other links in one central location. Users are able to modify both link entries and the topics by which all links are organized.

Stack:

  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • MongoDB
  • Mongoose
  • Materialize
